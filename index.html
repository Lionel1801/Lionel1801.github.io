About Me

“The goal is to turn data into information, and information into insight.” - Carly Fiorina

I am a Junior Data Scientist with a background in Physics and also in the Mechanical Engineering industry, with the curiosity and imagination to make connections between different data sets, draw conclusions and turn problems into solutions. With my passion for making a sense of information, and turning data into knowledge, I retrained as a Data Scientist to improve my skills and be able to make sense of larger-scale data sets. I am currently in my last few modules to complete my Master’s in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Innovation Technology.

I am skilled in collecting and manipulating data, designing, and testing hypothesis, creating predictive models, storytelling about the findings, as well as in building and designing self-running AI systems to automate predictive models. I thrive in fast-paced, and goal-oriented environments with dedicated teams as well as working independently. I am passionate about simplifying problematic and complex business questions, ideas, and real-life scenarios, using analytical skills.
